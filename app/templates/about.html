open sourced inspiration

I was genuinely inspired by what Quynh and Toan achieved with lovetheworkmore. I truly was. Inspired enough to take their concept further. Because for us —the "young, broke, and passionate" creatives-, the challenge isn't just a limited budget for LoveTheWork subscriptions, but also the lack of resources to gamble with our work.

And that's precisely where LoveTheWorkMoreMore steps in. By typing an -ironic- extra More, you are now able to find winner, shortlisted and unawarded campaigns dating back to 2017. With filters for you to study each Lion, Section and Category, this simple tool will help you learn from creatives' past triumphs and missteps —since failure is a stern teacher, after all!

By the way, I wont paint LoveTheWork as an elitist adversary. In my conversations with them, I've encountered nothing but passion and kindness. If you're in a position to do so, by all means, subscribe to LoveTheWork. They offer a lot more than just case studies.

But if you are like me, and the subscription isn't within reach, consider joining our open-source community of ad nerds and broke creatives.

Let's build a new community, driving inspiration and feeling inspired along the way.
